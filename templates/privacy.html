Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Spendly ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our personal expense tracking application ("Service"). By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly, as well as data generated through your use of the Service, including:

  • Account information, such as your name and email address
  • Expense data you log, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions
  • Basic usage information, such as login timestamps and device/browser type

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service
  • Generate your expense summaries, categories, and spending insights
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Improve and troubleshoot the Service

We do not use your expense data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in our application database and retained for as long as your account remains active. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time, subject to any legal or operational retention requirements.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on third-party services (such as font or infrastructure providers) to operate the Service. These providers may process limited technical data, such as your IP address, solely to deliver their service to us. We do not share your expense data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through the Spendly support channels.
